Have a topic you'd like the club to explore, or know someone who would make a
great speaker? Let us know — the speaker organizer tracks all requests and
tries to schedule them within a few months.

### Suggestions welcome

Past successful programs have covered local history, environmental topics,
career journeys, community initiatives, and student achievements. There's no
wrong idea — if it would interest a room of engaged community members, it's
worth suggesting.

### Rotarians: nominate a fellow member

If you'd like to see a fellow Rotarian share what they do professionally or as
a volunteer, submit their name via the form above.
